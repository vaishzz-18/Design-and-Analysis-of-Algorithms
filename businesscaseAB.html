Pollution Monitoring

This business case focuses on real-time monitoring of pollution levels using sensors and automated systems. The aim is to ensure timely detection and management of pollution in urban areas, thus protecting public health.

Union-Find Algorithm: Utilized to manage and efficiently group different pollution monitoring stations, enabling the identification of pollution clusters across the city.

Implementation: Data from sensors is processed to detect and address pollution hotspots. The Union-Find algorithm helps in grouping areas with similar pollution levels for targeted interventions.

Feedback Collection and Display

This business case involves collecting feedback from citizens about city services and displaying this feedback to the relevant authorities. It aims to enhance service delivery and citizen satisfaction by addressing issues based on public input.

Disjoint Set Union (DSU): Used to efficiently manage and display feedback data by grouping similar feedback points, which helps in identifying common issues raised by citizens.

Implementation: Feedback is collected through surveys and online platforms, grouped using DSU for efficient display, and analyzed to improve city services.
