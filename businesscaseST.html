Resort Recommendation

This case study focuses on building a machine learning model to recommend suitable resorts to users based on their preferences and travel dates. The model will consider factors like budget, desired amenities, and travel season to provide personalized recommendations.

This case study explores the design and implementation of a secure and efficient payment processing system. It will cover aspects like fraud detection, payment gateway integration, and ensuring customer data privacy while facilitating seamless online transactions.
